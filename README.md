### Hi, I'm Vishnu Vardhan

Software Engineer based in **Texas, USA**. I architect, build, and modernize large-scale
enterprise systems — and guide the teams that ship them. I work from first principles: I'd
rather understand something by building it than by importing it, and I've learned the hard
part is rarely the code — it's the judgment about what to keep simple, what to make robust,
and what to leave out.

#### What I do
- 🧩 **Solve the hard problems** — I dig past symptoms to the real root cause, then find the simplest fix that actually holds. Some of my best work has been stabilizing live production systems under pressure and building tools on short notice to crack stubborn issues.
- 🏛️ **Architect end-to-end** — data, integration, and delivery, on systems that can't go down.
- 🔗 **Enterprise integration** — connect services across REST/SOAP, MuleSoft, and Kafka with reliable messaging and clean failure recovery.
- ⚙️ **Modernize & scale** — Spring Boot microservices on Docker/Kubernetes, CI/CD with quality gates, and high-volume batch tuned for throughput and reliability.
- 🤝 **Lead by lifting** — coach and mentor engineers, run honest reviews, and present clearly to leadership.

#### How I work
- 🧠 **Critical thinking first** — I question the requirement and the assumptions before writing code, and weigh tradeoffs out in the open.
- 🧱 **Simplicity over cleverness** — the best system is the smallest one that survives reality.
- 🔍 **Depth over hype** — know *how* something works, not just *that* it works. Lately: how AI & LLMs actually function — learned by building from scratch.
- 📦 **Ship & iterate** — small, reliable releases beat big, risky ones.
